HELP/TIPS:

  1. Press Help/Tips or esc button to close this menu.
  2. Guessing all the correct letters will solve the word.
  3. There is currently an issue where sometimes free letters are not accurate. The issue is sometimes a letter is selected twice and has no effect.
  4. There is moderate keyboard support: Enter = Submit, Backspace = Undo, all alphabet keys = Letter.
  5. If your guess attempt is wrong, you have to attempt a new word and are deducted a point.
  6. You are not deducted a point for pushing "New Word." Feel free to try for a word you can solve.
  7. Pressing "Guess Word" will enable the option to input a final word guess early.
  8. The timer starts when you use your last turn.
  9. You are given one less free letter per word at 3, 7, and 10 points.
  10. The letters are touch-screen friendly.
  11. Currently working on new words and word types!
