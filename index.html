Help/Tips:
-The words were generated by AI and are challenging.

-It is possible to enter a letter already given to you "BE CAREFUL."

-The letters are not touch screen, you will need to input your letters and press enter.

-press "Guess word" to enable Text box that will allow you to attmpt final word guess. press that same button to return to picking letters.

-you will automatically be forced to guess final word after reaching max turns, you will not fail the word when reaching max turns.

-You are given 1 less Free letter per word at 3, 7, and 10 points.

-You are given 2 extra Free letters at -3 points.

- if your guess attempt is wrong you have to attempt a new word and are deducted a point.

-You are not deducted a point for pushing "new word."
